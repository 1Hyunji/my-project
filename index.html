Beet Soup

Beet soup isn’t just delicious, it’s also jam packed with nutrients. A mix of beets, parsnip, ginger, onion and garlic, this blended version is creamy, savory, and rich in flavor. Top it off with a swirl of coconut cream (or yogurt), parsley, and black sesame seeds.

Super-sweet corn curry

This Gujarati Sweetcorn Curry recipe or Kasoli and Jugu nu Saak is an East African recipe and is made by Gujarati Indians who grew up in East Africa. My Aunties taught my Mum this recipe many years ago and it is one of our top favourite curries!
